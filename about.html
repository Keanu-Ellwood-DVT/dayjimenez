Bio

Day is a designer who creates simple and delightful experiences to improve everyday life. His work has ranged in scale, from digital interfaces, identities, and communications to spatial environments. Day has built and led teams through the creative, development, and marketing processes to reach hundreds of millions of people.

Currently

Day provides independent product design and product management services. He specializes on an end-to-end product process, from business insights through implementation, and advises companies on product and collaboration practices.
