Bio

Day enjoys solving problems and improving human experiences. His work ranges in scale, from architecture and environments to digital interaction and brand identity. Day has built and led teams through the creative process, technical production, and market distribution. Today, he is focused on designing products and the people who use them.

Currently

Day provides indepenedent product design and product management services. He specializes on an end-to-end product process, from business insights to implementation, and advises companies on improving their product practice.
